RepAnvil Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

RepAnvil (“the app”) is an offline fitness workout planner for Android, published by the RepAnvil developer. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how it is stored, and your choices. By using RepAnvil, you agree to this policy.

Summary

  • No account or sign-in is required.
  • No ads and no analytics or crash-reporting SDKs.
  • We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data with third parties.
  • The app does not connect to RepAnvil servers and does not request network permissions in the release build.
  • Workout and app data stay on your device unless you choose to export a backup file.

1. Information the app stores on your device

RepAnvil stores data locally on your phone or tablet to provide its features. This information is not transmitted to us and is not uploaded automatically.

  • Workout programs and training-day configurations you create, edit, or clone
  • Exercise library entries, including custom exercises you add
  • Workout session logs, set logs, and personal records
  • Body metric entries you choose to record
  • App preferences such as theme mode and onboarding choices

You may enter fitness-related information (for example, weight lifted or body weight). You control what you enter. We do not require your real name, email address, or phone number to use the app.

2. How we use information

Local data is used only to:

  • Display and manage your workout programs and exercises
  • Log training sessions and calculate progress metrics such as estimated one-rep max
  • Save your app settings and preferences
  • Export or restore a backup when you initiate those actions

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing.

3. Data sharing

RepAnvil does not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for their own purposes. The app does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or social-login services.

If you use Export backup, you choose where the JSON file is saved through the Android system file picker. Any sharing after export is under your control.

4. Backup, restore, and deletion

User-initiated backup: You may export a JSON backup to a location you select. Importing a backup replaces local app data with the contents of that file. RepAnvil does not upload backups automatically.

Android system backup: The app may participate in Android’s optional device backup feature when enabled on your device (for example, through your Google account settings). That process is managed by Android, not by RepAnvil servers.

Delete your data: You can remove all app data at any time via Settings → Reset all data inside the app, or by uninstalling the app. Uninstalling removes locally stored app data from your device.

5. Permissions

RepAnvil is designed to run with no Android runtime permissions in the release build. The app does not request access to the Internet, location, camera, microphone, contacts, notifications, Bluetooth, or broad storage access.

Backup and restore uses the Android Storage Access Framework (system file picker). You choose which file to read or write; the app does not need legacy storage permissions for this.

6. Children’s privacy

RepAnvil is a general fitness tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will assist with deletion on the device.

7. Security

Data is stored in the app’s private storage area on your device, protected by Android’s application sandbox. Because the app works offline and does not transmit data to our servers, security also depends on how you protect your device (screen lock, backups, and file access).

8. International users

RepAnvil processes data only on your device. No cross-border transfer to RepAnvil occurs because we do not operate backend servers that receive your workout data.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest version. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy. Material changes may also be noted in the app or on the Google Play store listing when appropriate.

10. Google Play Data safety

For Google Play’s Data safety section, RepAnvil:

  • Does not collect or share user data with third parties
  • Stores fitness and app-preference data locally for app functionality only
  • Does not require data collection to use core features
  • Allows users to delete local data through in-app reset or by uninstalling the app
